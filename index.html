Sarah (Hyojin) Lee

I'm a ML engineer at Digital Health Team, Samsung Electronics in South Korea. My Research interest lies in developing machine learning model for monitoring and improving general health, especially sleep.

At Digital Health Team, I am developing ML algorithms for sleep apnea and sleep staging. Also, I am working on developing health foundation models for diverse health-related downstream tasks using wearable sensor data. I am part of Samsung Sponsored Research with MIT Media Lab which includes Sleep Entrainment and Digital Biomarker for Sleep.

Previously, I did my Masters at Seoul National University Graduate School of Data Science, where I was advised by Hyung-Sin Kim. I also co-advised by Hyun-woo Shin, a professor in Seoul National University College of Medicine/Seoul National University Hospital.

Before that, I received Bachelor's degree in Biology and Business Administration in Seoul National University.
